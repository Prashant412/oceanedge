Pre-Purchase Inspection

A pre-purchase inspection is a comprehensive and rigorous process that may require several days to complete. It involves a thorough assessment of the vessel's structural integrity, watertightness, installed machinery and equipment, and overall operating condition. Our company specializes in conducting pre-purchase and independent vessel condition surveys on behalf of our clients, offering cost-effective rates. We diligently identify any deficiencies found during the inspection and provide detailed recommendations to our clients.
